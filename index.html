Our Experience

Refusal to Pay ER Visit Bills

IMG refused to pay for an emergency room visit, citing a pre-existing condition. This was despite their policy stating coverage for pre-existing acute onset conditions.

Delays in Processing Claims

We experienced significant delays in claim processing, often exceeding promised response times by months.

Concealment of Claims Information

All our claims were hidden from IMG's website, making it difficult to manage and process our claims.

Lack of Regulatory Support

Complaints to the Indiana Department of Insurance went unanswered.

Financial Impact

We now face daily calls from collections and hospitals, causing significant stress and potentially impacting our credit.
